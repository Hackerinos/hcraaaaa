Frequently asked questions

What if I decide I want a different plan?

Our automated system will upgrade your account instantly with no data loss. Your limits will be unlocked within a few minutes and you will receive a confirmation email.

What are my payment options?

We currently accept Stripe, and Crypto payments for our upgrades. We only use certified FCAA approved payment gateways equipped with EWP to prevent fraud.

Are there contracts or commitments?

We believe in honesty and integrity, we do not make you agree to anything besides our Terms of Service. We have been sponsored and paid off by our generous donors. We only charge for upgrades for general maintenance and our development teams costs.

Do I need to enter my credit card to get a free trial?

You do not need to enter any financial details, only when you make your one time payment. All we ask for is registration so you can integrate our system with ease!
